About Me
Hello! My name is JC and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2022 when my special friend showed me the fun and potential of this profession — turns out enrolling in Zuitt Web Development Course taught me a lot about HTML, CSS and Bootstrap!
Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at an advertising agency, a start-up, an e-commerce app, and a large fast-food company. My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences at Upwork for a variety of clients.
Outside of work I keep my self fit and active to help me maintain concentration levels during working hours. Best time to program is when having coffee while raining (:
